# SolaRPlexus Showcase Submission

## Project headline

SolaRPlexus: a resume analyzer that finds your best-fit job and builds a tailored application kit.

## Short description

SolaRPlexus helps job seekers understand which roles fit their resume best. Users paste their resume, enter a target job and company, and receive role-fit recommendations, a hiring-manager brief, an evidence ledger, tailored resume bullets, missing keyword guidance, interview prompts, a company-specific cover letter, a recruiter outreach message, and AI-trainer proof artifacts including a preference rationale, evaluation scorecard, error taxonomy, and rubric matrix.

## What I built

I built a responsive web app with resume analysis, job-family scoring, role match cards, recruiter scan, hiring-manager brief, evidence ledger, signal bars, a fit radar chart, tailored bullet generation, ATS keyword planning, skill-gap recommendations, cover letter drafting, recruiter outreach drafting, cover-letter authenticity audit, AI response-evaluation proof lab, model evaluation scorecard, showcase proof pack, error taxonomy, rubric calibration matrix, interview drill, application guardrails, local autosave, and markdown export.

## Why it matters

Students are often told to tailor their resume, but they are not always shown how. This project turns vague career advice into a concrete workflow: identify fit, prove fit, close gaps, and write application materials that connect real experience to a specific company.

## Responsible AI notes

The prototype runs locally and does not send resume text to a third-party service. It avoids hidden scoring and shows the candidate what matched, what is missing, and what should only be added if they have real evidence.

## Future version

The next version would add saved job applications, PDF parsing, custom role libraries, and optional LLM-assisted rewriting with an edit history.
