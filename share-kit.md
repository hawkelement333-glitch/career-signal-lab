# SolaRPlexus Share Kit

## Handshake or LinkedIn post

I built SolaRPlexus, a resume analyzer that helps job seekers find their best-fit role and tailor an application for a specific company.

The app lets you paste a resume, enter a target job title and company, and receive role-fit recommendations, a hiring-manager brief, an evidence ledger, matched and missing keywords, tailored resume bullets, a skill-gap plan, interview prompts, a recruiter scan, a company-specific cover letter, a recruiter outreach message, and AI-trainer proof artifacts with a preference rationale, model-evaluation scorecard, error taxonomy, and calibration rubric. I gave it a cinematic SolaRPlexus-style interface so the project feels polished and memorable while keeping the analysis transparent and editable.

Skills shown: product thinking, resume analysis, recommendation logic, career coaching UX, responsible AI design, model-response evaluation, data visualization, and frontend implementation.

## Resume bullet

## 30-second demo script

"SolaRPlexus helps job seekers tailor applications without losing their authentic voice. I paste in resume notes, add a job title and company, and the app recommends the best-fit role family. It shows the evidence behind the match, creates a hiring-manager brief, separates defensible claims from risky ones, highlights missing keywords, creates stronger resume bullets, drafts recruiter outreach, prepares interview prompts, audits the cover letter for believability, and generates AI-trainer proof artifacts: a preference rationale, scorecard, error taxonomy, and rubric. The goal is to make career tailoring feel clear, honest, and actionable."
