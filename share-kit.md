# SolaRPlexus Project Moonlight Share Kit

## Handshake or LinkedIn post

I built SolaRPlexus Project Moonlight, a resume analyzer that helps job seekers upload a resume, find their best-fit role, and tailor an application for any company.

The app lets you upload or paste a resume, enter any target company and job title, and receive role-fit recommendations, a company match brief, a hiring-manager brief, an evidence ledger, matched and missing keywords, tailored resume bullets, a skill-gap plan, interview prompts, a recruiter scan, a company-specific cover letter, a recruiter outreach message, and AI-trainer proof artifacts with a preference rationale, model-evaluation scorecard, error taxonomy, and calibration rubric. I gave it a cinematic SolaRPlexus-style interface so the project feels polished and memorable while keeping the analysis transparent and editable.

Skills shown: product thinking, resume analysis, recommendation logic, career coaching UX, responsible AI design, model-response evaluation, data visualization, and frontend implementation.
